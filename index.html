About Me

I am in the second year of my DPhil (PhD) in Sociology, at the University of Oxford. My research is focussed on political sociology, using quantitative methods to understand how individuals come to hold certain attitudes, identify with some groups but not others, and vote for one party over another.

In my spare time I like playing tennis, ranting about how great Linux is to anyone who will listen, and re-watching the The Office (UK version of course) more times than I care to publicly admit.
