Welcome to my website! Here you can find updates on my research, a copy of my CV, and my contact details. I also have a short blog.

I am a currently studying for a DPhil (PhD) at the University of Oxford, focussed on political sociology. I am based at Nuffield College. My supervisor is Steve Fisher, and I receive my funding from the ESRC.

My main academic interest is political sociology, specifically why individuals vote the way they do and how they come to form the attitudes they hold. Campaign and media effects, in the Brexit referendum and recent British elections, form the basis of my current research.
